# Output Marketing — Product, Services, and Marketing System

## What Output Marketing Is

Output Marketing is a marketing agency focused mainly on B2B SaaS companies.

We help lean teams execute content, SEO, founder and brand content, launch marketing, and reporting without building a full in-house department.

We are not positioning ourselves as:
- a generalist agency for every industry
- a paid ads shop
- an influencer-first agency
- a video production company
- a solo freelancer offer

## Core Positioning

We help B2B SaaS companies turn content, visibility, and launches into pipeline.
