# Business Plan: Boutique Marketing Agency 2026
**Target market:** B2B SaaS & DTC eCommerce — US (California focus) & Canada
**Team:** Vanessa Semprun (Influencer Marketing, Social Media, Content, SEO) + Angel (AI-powered ops, landing pages, written content strategy)

---

## The Opportunity

AI tools made it cheap to produce content. They also made most of it sound the same. B2B buyers, especially in SaaS, are sorting through decks and emails and LinkedIn posts that could have been written by the same program. Because they were.

55% of consumers trust brands more when their content is visibly human-made. Google rewards Experience, Expertise, Authority, and Trust — things that don't come from a prompt. MarTech put it plainly: "human connection is marketing's real advantage in 2026."

The brands paying attention are already acting on it.

---

## Positioning

**"B2B marketing, but make it human."**

We help B2B SaaS startups and DTC eCommerce brands build influencer programs, content strategies, and social presence that read like a person wrote them. Because one did.

We're not a 50-person agency with a project manager sitting between you and whoever is doing the actual work. We're a handful of people who do things really well. You talk directly to the people working on your account.

### What We Don't Do

We don't run paid ads. We don't produce video. We don't have a floor of junior coordinators running your account. We focus on influencer marketing, content strategy, LinkedIn, and SEO. These we do well, consistently, without the overhead that makes large agencies slow and expensive.
