| `angel-photo.jpg` | JPG | Min 800x800px. Real photo, same tone as Vanessa's. Professional but not stiff. | /about Angel section |

**Photo direction note for Vanessa:**
In both photos, Vanessa's gaze or body angle should orient toward the right side of the frame. On the homepage and about page, the CTA sits to her left — her eyeline or body position should draw attention toward it, not away from it. This is a documented CRO technique. If you only have one good photo, use it for both placements.

**What "not a headshot" means:**
She should not be looking directly at the camera on a plain background. A desk, a laptop, a real workspace — anything that shows she's working, not posing. Candid-adjacent.

**Fallback:** If only one Vanessa photo exists, use `vanessa-hero.jpg` for both slots.
