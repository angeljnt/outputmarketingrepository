# How We Package It

## 30-Day Pilot

Every qualified lead should ideally start here.

Price:
- $1,500 to $2,000

Purpose:
- Audit current state
- Identify what is broken
- Show how we work
- Reduce risk before a longer engagement

Includes:
- Current-state audit
- Strategic roadmap
- 2 to 3 weeks of live execution in the highest-priority channel
- End-of-pilot recommendations

Core message:
- Pilot first
- Month-to-month if we continue
- No annual contract

- Founder-led B2B SaaS companies with no marketing hire

# Who We Are Best For

## Primary ICP

### B2B SaaS and AI SaaS

Best fit overall.

Why they fit:
- Lean teams
- Clear growth pressure
- Need for content, visibility, and launches
- Strong fit for content, SEO, LinkedIn, and reporting

### Revenue / Sales / Customer Software

Very strong commercial fit.

Why they fit:
- Easier to connect work to pipeline
- Usually think in GTM terms
- Strong commercial fit for content and launches

### Workflow / Ops / Automation Software

Strong fit when the product is technical and the team needs help translating product value into market-facing execution.

### Analytics / Data / Infrastructure Software

Strong fit when the company needs better technical-to-market translation, stronger content, and clearer distribution.

## Secondary ICP
- Boutique agencies needing white-label support
- B2B service businesses with active marketing motion
- Education / EdTech with clear content motion
- Selected DTC brands only when the fit is clearly content and social execution

## Low Priority / Excluded
- Staffing and recruiting firms
- Consulting-heavy firms
- Media, newsletter, or community-led businesses
- Influencer-first companies
- Large enterprises with obvious in-house capacity
- Very early startups with no marketing motion
