Start date: 2026-03-23
Context: Auditing the Output Marketing 12-page website wireframe against CRO best practices. Constraint: no testimonials available. All proof must come from logos, awards, case study metrics, credentials, and the damaging admission.

## Key questions to answer
1. What CRO elements are we missing across the 12 pages?
2. Are there proven trust-building patterns we haven't used yet (that don't require testimonials)?
3. Is the contact form optimally designed?
4. Are the CTAs positioned and worded at the highest-converting standard?
5. Is the FAQ covering the right objections in the right order?
6. Are there proven scroll depth or page length optimizations to apply?
7. What does the research say about pricing page structure specifically?
8. What micro-copy signals (trust lines, risk reducers) are we missing?
