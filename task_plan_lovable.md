## Goal
1. Produce a single Lovable build prompt that covers all 12 pages with full copy, design system, and CRO specs — structured so Lovable replies only by requesting attachments before it builds.
2. Produce a CRO justification document citing the research behind every structural and design decision on the site.

## Constraints
- No mention of Airwallex, Apollo, or CreatorBuzz anywhere
- Anti-ai-prose rules apply to all copy

## Deliverables

Complete build brief for Lovable. Sections:

- Design system (colors, typography, spacing, component patterns)

### 2. lovable_attachments/ATTACHMENTS_NEEDED.md
Master checklist for every visual asset needed. Each entry has:
- Exact filename to use
- Format and dimension specs
- Where it appears on the site
- Notes on what the image should show

### 3. cro_justification.md
Decision-by-decision breakdown of every CRO principle applied, with:
- The decision made
- The research/source that supports it
- How it shows up on the Output Marketing site
