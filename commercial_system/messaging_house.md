# Messaging House

## Category

Output Marketing is a B2B SaaS content and organic growth agency.

## Primary audience

- B2B SaaS founders
- lean B2B SaaS marketing teams
- Series A and Series B teams that need content to drive pipeline

## Secondary audience

- boutique agencies that need white-label execution capacity
- selected adjacent businesses with strong content motion

## Core problem

Most B2B SaaS companies publish content without turning it into authority, demand capture, or measurable pipeline.

## Core promise

We turn founder voice, company content, SEO, and measurement into an organic growth system that is easier to buy than building a full in-house team.

## Positioning statement

Output Marketing helps B2B SaaS founders and lean marketing teams build organic pipeline through founder LinkedIn, company content, SEO, and measurement, starting with a 30-day pilot and scaling into a focused month-to-month program.

## Messaging pillars

### 1. Pipeline, not vanity

We do not sell content as output alone. We sell content as an organic pipeline system.

### 2. Founder voice plus company engine

The best-performing motion is not founder content alone or company content alone. It is both, working together.

### 3. Focus beats breadth

We do not run paid ads, video production, PR, and every other channel. Narrow focus is the reason the work stays sharp.

### 4. Pilot first

The `30-Day Pilot` reduces buying risk and creates clarity before a longer engagement.

### 5. Direct operators

Clients talk directly to the people doing the work.

## Differentiators

- Pilot-first commercial model
- B2B SaaS-first positioning
- Real proof in launches, content systems, and reporting discipline
- Small, senior, execution-heavy team
- White-label capability without turning the whole agency into a generic backend vendor

## Approved language

## Objection handling

### "We already publish content."

The issue is usually not publishing. It is whether the content is connected to authority, demand capture, and measurement.

### "We only need SEO."

SEO works best when it is tied to content quality and distribution. Standalone SEO is usually a temporary state, not the best end state.

### "Do you only work with SaaS?"

The public offer is built for B2B SaaS. We also support a limited number of agency partners through white-label scopes.

### "Do you do white-label?"

Yes, selectively. It is a real offer, but it is custom and secondary to the core SaaS growth offer.
