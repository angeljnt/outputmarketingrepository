# Packages Master

## Package architecture

### Core public packages

- `30-Day Pilot`
- `Foundation`
- `Engine`
- `Full Stack`

### Secondary custom offers

- `White-Label / Capacity Track`
- `Project Track`

### Public add-ons

- `Second founder LinkedIn ghostwriting`
- `pSEO setup`
- `pSEO monthly maintenance`
- `GEO`
- `Content audit`
- `SEO technical audit`
- `Positioning + messaging audit`

## Packaging rules

- Do not create a new public package for every service family.
- Use the core public packages for the main B2B SaaS growth offer.
- Use `White-Label / Capacity Track` and `Project Track` for real but secondary demand.
- White-label should be publicly discoverable, but custom and secondary.

## Package summary

| Package | Status | Best fit | Core modules | Natural next move |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `30-Day Pilot` | Core public | Qualified prospects that need fit validation before a retainer | audit, roadmap, 2 to 3 weeks of live execution, debrief | `Foundation`, `Engine`, `Full Stack`, or `Project Track` |
| `Foundation` | Core public | Founder-led B2B SaaS with no marketing hire | founder content, light SEO base, content calendar, reporting | `Engine` |
| `Engine` | Core public | Lean B2B SaaS team with PMF and organic pipeline pressure | founder content, company content, SEO cadence, KPI reporting | `Full Stack` |
| `Full Stack` | Core public | Series A/B team that needs measurement, distribution, and ROI visibility | everything in `Engine` plus technical SEO, distribution, attribution, research asset | add-ons or long-term scale |
| `White-Label / Capacity Track` | Secondary custom | Boutique agencies or fractional teams that need execution capacity | embedded delivery across content, SEO, reporting, and support workflows | ongoing custom scope |
| `Project Track` | Secondary custom | Audits, pSEO builds, launch sprints, or adjacent scoped work | scoped strategy or execution sprint | core package or maintenance scope |

## 30-Day Pilot

### Best for

- new prospects with real need but unclear package destination
- prospects who want to see how the team works before committing
- adjacent or more complex accounts that need diagnosis first

### Solves

- buying risk
- unclear channel priority
- low-confidence packaging decisions
- mismatch between stated need and actual need

### Includes

- current-state audit
- clear priority roadmap
- 2 to 3 weeks of live execution
- end-of-pilot recommendation

### Not ideal for

- free-strategy seekers
- inactive or non-urgent companies
- teams expecting a complete marketing transformation in 30 days

### Proof required

- speed
- judgment
- clarity
- quality of execution in a short window

## Foundation

### Best for

- seed-stage B2B SaaS founders
- companies without a marketing hire
- teams that need presence and consistency before they need scale

### Core modules

- 12 ghostwritten LinkedIn posts per month
- 2 SEO articles per month
- content calendar
- monthly performance report
- 1 strategy call per month

### Solves

- inconsistent founder visibility
- weak content foundation
- no publishing rhythm
- no simple reporting loop

### Not ideal for

- teams already under pressure to prove content-driven pipeline
- accounts that need brand-page motion and broader company content immediately
- white-label or project-only scopes

### Upsell path

- `Engine`
- messaging audit
- content audit
- second founder ghostwriting

### Proof needed

- strong founder voice capture
- content consistency
- early-stage credibility

## Engine

### Best for

- lean B2B SaaS teams with PMF
- companies that need organic pipeline, not just presence
- teams where founder voice and company content should work together

### Core modules

- founder LinkedIn ghostwriting
- brand-page management
- 4 to 6 SEO articles per month
- monthly KPI reporting
- quarterly strategy session

### Solves

- disconnected founder and company content
- weak content cadence
- weak demand capture
- reporting without a commercial story

### Not ideal for

- companies too early for a recurring content system
- companies that already need attribution and distribution depth from day one
- agency partners needing capacity, not a SaaS growth retainer

### Upsell path

- `Full Stack`
- GEO
- pSEO
- second founder ghostwriting

### Proof needed

- recurring content system proof
- reporting proof
- proof that content can support pipeline, not just publishing volume

## Full Stack

### Best for

- Series A/B teams
- teams under pressure to prove content ROI
- companies with enough maturity to operationalize attribution and distribution

### Core modules

- everything in `Engine`
- 8 articles per month
- technical SEO audit plus optimization
- distribution layer
- attribution setup
- 1 original research asset per quarter

### Solves

- inability to defend content investment
- weak distribution of strong content
- technical SEO debt
- missing attribution and measurement discipline

### Not ideal for

- very early teams
- companies that cannot support measurement infrastructure
- buyers who only need a one-off build or sprint

### Upsell path

- pSEO
- GEO
- second founder ghostwriting

### Proof needed

- measurement and reporting proof
- multi-channel execution proof
- stronger evidence than content-only case studies

## White-Label / Capacity Track

### Market posture

Publicly discoverable. Custom by design. Secondary to the core B2B SaaS growth offer.

### Best for

- boutique agencies
- fractional teams
- service partners with sold work but limited delivery capacity

### Core modules

- backend content execution
- SEO support
- reporting support
- embedded delivery cadence
- QA-aware collaboration

### Not ideal for

- agencies that only want strategy
- agencies that need fully anonymous delivery without collaboration
- companies that really fit the public SaaS packages better

### Proof needed

- white-label-style writing proof
- reliability
- adaptability across accounts
- evidence of operating well inside another team's workflow

## Project Track

### Best for

- messaging audits
- pSEO builds
- launch sprints
- technical SEO audits
- adjacent cases not ready for a retainer

### Core modules

- scoped diagnostic
- scoped execution sprint
- clearly bounded deliverables
- follow-on recommendation

### Not ideal for

- companies that obviously need a recurring engine
- buyers trying to avoid a better-fitting retainer

### Proof needed

- launch proof
- sprint proof
- delivery speed
- clarity of recommendations

## Add-on logic

| Add-on | Best attached to | Use when |
|---|---|---|
| `Second founder LinkedIn ghostwriting` | `Engine`, `Full Stack` | more than one executive voice matters |
| `pSEO setup` | `Full Stack`, `Project Track` | there is a large template, use-case, integration, or comparison surface |
| `pSEO monthly maintenance` | `Full Stack` | the pSEO build is already live |
| `GEO` | `Engine`, `Full Stack` | the team wants AI citation visibility on top of the core engine |
| `Content audit` | any package | content exists, but needs cleanup before scale |
| `SEO technical audit` | `Full Stack`, `Project Track` | technical issues are the bottleneck |
| `Positioning + messaging audit` | `30-Day Pilot`, `Foundation`, `Project Track` | the message is too weak to scale output cleanly |
