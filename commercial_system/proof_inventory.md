# Proof Inventory

This file maps historical proof to current Output Marketing services and packages.

Important guardrails:
- No historical account is described here as literally buying today's `Foundation`, `Engine`, or `Full Stack` packages.
- Package labels below mean `equivalent proof relevance`, not `literal historical purchase`.
- When the evidence is thin, the entry stays modular or is explicitly excluded from strong package claims.

## Package shorthand

- `Foundation`: founder-led LinkedIn plus light content plus basic reporting.
- `Engine`: recurring content system plus organic demand capture plus KPI reporting.
- `Full Stack`: `Engine` plus broader distribution, measurement/ops, and heavier content infrastructure.
- `Project Track`: launch sprints, pilots, audits, newsletter programs, or one-off campaigns.
- `White-Label / Capacity Track`: white-label, overflow execution, agency-side writing, research, or support work.
