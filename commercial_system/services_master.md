# Services Master

## Objective

Turn Output Marketing's delivery capability into a productized service system instead of a loose list of tasks.

## Service-system rules

- Organize services by buying logic, not by isolated deliverables.
- Keep the public offer narrower than the internal capability.
- Treat white-label as real, but secondary.
- Keep paid media, video production, PR, and influencer-led work outside the active catalog.

## Service families

| Service family | Status | What it solves | Typical deliverables | Sell when | Do not lead with it when | Package relation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `Strategy` | `Core entry` | Lack of clarity on ICP, message, channel priority, or sequencing | audit, roadmap, positioning review, content plan, keyword architecture | the buyer needs diagnostic clarity before committing | the buyer only wants cheap strategy or abstract advisory | built into `30-Day Pilot`; can expand through audits |
| `Founder / Executive Content` | `Core public` | Low founder visibility and weak authority in-market | voice capture, ghostwritten posts, carousels, editorial calendar, performance review | founder voice is a commercial growth lever | the founder will not participate at all | core to `Foundation` and `Engine` |
| `SEO & Organic Growth` | `Core public` | Low organic visibility, weak demand capture, or no compounding content motion | keyword architecture, content optimization, technical recommendations, GEO, pSEO strategy | the company already has real buyer demand to capture | the company wants instant paid-like results | core to `Engine` and `Full Stack`; several public add-ons |
| `Content Production` | `Core public` | Strategy exists, but publishing quality or consistency is weak | SEO articles, landing pages, content refreshes, case-study assets, newsletters | the client needs recurring output tied to a real content thesis | the ask is pure volume with no strategic spine | embedded in `Foundation`, `Engine`, and `Full Stack` |
| `Distribution & Repurposing` | `Core advanced` | Good content exists but is under-distributed | email repurposing, brand-page distribution, carousel adaptation, channel-specific reuse | content quality is already solid and reach is now the bottleneck | the base content engine is still weak | part of `Full Stack`; selective add-on support elsewhere |
| `Measurement & Attribution` | `Core advanced` | The team cannot defend content investment or connect output to pipeline | KPI reporting, GA4/CRM attribution setup, reporting structure, review cadence | multiple stakeholders care about ROI and accountability | the client is too early to instrument properly | advanced `Full Stack` capability |
| `Launch & GTM Support` | `Secondary custom` | Product releases or campaigns need sharper execution and coordination | launch messaging, webinar support, campaign rollout, launch sprint execution | the company has clear launch motion and needs extra execution | the client needs a full public package replacement | best sold through `Project Track` or as a scoped add-on |
| `White-Label / Embedded Support` | `Secondary custom` | Agencies or lean teams need reliable execution capacity | backend content delivery, SEO support, reporting, embedded execution support | agency partners need delivery capacity without more payroll | the public SaaS growth offer is already the right fit | sold through `White-Label / Capacity Track` |
| `Creative Support` | `Secondary custom` | Written work needs companion visual assets | simple social graphics, email assets, carousel design support, content formatting | visuals are supporting a content system already in motion | the buyer mainly wants a design or video shop | secondary custom support only |
| `Add-ons` | `Public add-on` | The buyer needs targeted leverage without a whole new package | second founder ghostwriting, pSEO, GEO, content audit, technical SEO audit, messaging audit | a specific constraint or opportunity is clear | the add-on is being used to avoid fixing core fit | bolts onto the core packages |

## What is public vs custom

### Core public offer

- `30-Day Pilot`
- `Foundation`
- `Engine`
- `Full Stack`

### Secondary custom offer

- `White-Label / Capacity Track`
- `Project Track`
- scoped launch support
- embedded reporting or execution support

### Public add-ons

- `Second founder LinkedIn ghostwriting`
- `pSEO setup`
- `pSEO monthly maintenance`
- `GEO`
- `Content audit`
- `SEO technical audit`
- `Positioning + messaging audit`

## Explicitly inactive or out of scope

- paid acquisition management
- full-service social media for broad consumer brands
- video production as a lead offer
- PR-led scopes
- influencer marketing as a current product line

## Operating use

Use this document to decide:

- what the agency really sells
