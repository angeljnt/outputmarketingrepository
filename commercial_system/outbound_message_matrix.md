# Outbound Message Matrix

Use this matrix to keep outbound tied to package fit, proof, and positioning.

| Segment | Buyer | Primary pain | Best package | Best proof angle | Opening angle | CTA | Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| B2B SaaS founder with no marketing hire | Founder / CEO | no consistent visibility or content base | `Foundation` | founder-content discipline and editorial consistency | "You have a product story worth hearing, but no system turning it into weekly visibility." | `30-Day Pilot` | over-selling attribution or a full content machine too early |
| Lean B2B SaaS marketing team | Head of Marketing / Growth | content exists but does not drive pipeline | `Engine` | Archive ongoing programs and reporting discipline | "You already publish. The missing layer is turning founder voice and company content into an engine." | `30-Day Pilot` | generic content-volume language |
| Revenue / sales software company | Head of Marketing / Demand Gen | demos and pipeline pressure | `Engine` | GTM-friendly proof and recurring content system | "Your buyers already research before they book. The question is whether your content shows up when it matters." | `30-Day Pilot` | brand-only or awareness-only framing |
| Workflow / automation software company | Product Marketing / Content Lead | technical product is not translating cleanly to the market | `Engine` | product-translation and launch support proof | "Complex products need clearer market-facing execution, not more internal jargon." | `30-Day Pilot` | selling only SEO with no messaging layer |
| Analytics / data / infrastructure company | Product Marketing / Head of Marketing | authority, technical SEO, and ROI visibility | `Full Stack` | measurement and multi-layer proof | "You likely have enough subject-matter depth already. The gap is packaging it into authority, distribution, and measurable demand." | `30-Day Pilot` | entry-level package language |
| Boutique agency partner | Agency Owner / Ops | sold work, limited delivery capacity | `White-Label / Capacity Track` | white-label-style execution proof | "If your team keeps selling but delivery bandwidth is the bottleneck, we can plug in behind the scenes." | custom intro call or scoped brief | leading with the public SaaS package language |
