# Services and Outreach ICP

## Services

We help lean marketing teams get more done without building a full in-house department.

Most companies do not need a giant agency. They need a team that can plug into the work, understand the business fast, and execute across content, social, SEO, reporting, and launches without creating more overhead.

That is where we fit.

### What We Do

| Service | What It Includes | Best Fit For | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| Content Marketing | Blog writing, thought leadership, landing page copy, editorial calendars, content refreshes, multi-post content production | B2B SaaS, agencies, education, service businesses | Consistent content output tied to traffic, leads, and positioning |
| SEO Content | SEO blog production, content strategy, landing page planning, optimization, large-scale publishing support | SaaS, content-heavy businesses, service companies | Better organic visibility and stronger content systems |
| Social Media Execution | Social strategy, post writing, publishing support, community management, weekly reporting, platform reviews | B2B brands, DTC brands, founder-led companies, service businesses | More consistent brand presence and cleaner day-to-day execution |
| Product and Launch Marketing | Feature launches, webinar support, campaign rollouts, launch messaging, cross-channel promotion | SaaS, AI tools, product-led companies | Better launch follow-through and stronger campaign execution |
| Marketing Ops and Reporting | KPI setup, attribution support, weekly reports, performance tracking, editorial systems, workflow cleanup | Teams with messy reporting or scattered execution | Better visibility into what is working and a more usable marketing system |
| White-Label Agency Support | Content production, SEO support, social execution, reporting, campaign support | Boutique agencies, fractional teams, outsourced marketing partners | Reliable execution capacity without adding payroll |
| Creative Support | Social graphics, email design, campaign asset support, content formatting | DTC brands, agencies, small marketing teams | Faster execution and more consistent visual output |

### Best-Fit Clients

- Growth-stage B2B SaaS companies
- Boutique agencies that need white-label execution
- Service businesses with active marketing and lean teams
- Selected DTC brands that need content and social support

## Clay Prompt

Build a prospect list of companies that fit this ICP for Output Marketing.

We are a marketing agency that helps lean teams execute content, SEO, social, launch marketing, reporting, and white-label marketing support without needing a full in-house department.

Prioritize these company types:

- B2B SaaS
- AI SaaS
- MarTech
- sales tech
- compliance / security tech
- workflow / operations software
- analytics / data software
- boutique marketing agencies
- staffing and recruiting firms
- education businesses
- B2B service businesses
- selected DTC ecommerce brands where the clear need is content and social execution

Prioritize these business conditions:

- Small or lean marketing teams
- Companies actively investing in content, SEO, webinars, product launches, case studies, and social
- Companies with visible marketing activity but likely needing more execution support
- Companies hiring for marketing-related roles
- Companies with active blogs, resource centers, webinars, case studies, and LinkedIn activity

Target geographies:

- United States
- Canada
- UAE

Company size:

- Prefer 10 to 200 employees
- Allow up to 500 employees if the company is clearly a strong fit

Exclude:

- Massive enterprises with large in-house teams
- Companies with no visible marketing activity
- Very early startups with no traction
- Translation / localization businesses
- Companies where influencer marketing is the most obvious service need
- Creator economy companies where creator partnerships are the primary pain point

Find decision-makers and likely buyers with these titles or similar:

- Founder
- Co-Founder
- CEO
- Head of Marketing
- VP Marketing
- Marketing Director
- Demand Generation Manager
- Growth Marketing Manager
- Head of Content
- Content Marketing Manager
- SEO Manager
- Social Media Manager

- RevOps Manager

For each company, return:

- Evidence / signal

Also label each lead with the most likely service fit:

## Search Logic

### Step 1: Find companies

- Search for companies in:
  - B2B SaaS
  - AI SaaS
  - MarTech
  - Sales tech
  - Compliance / security tech
  - Workflow / operations software
  - Analytics / data software
  - Boutique marketing agencies
  - Staffing and recruiting firms
  - Education businesses
  - B2B service businesses
  - Selected DTC ecommerce brands

### Step 2: Filter by size

- Primary range: 10 to 200 employees
- Secondary range: 201 to 500 employees only if strong fit

### Step 3: Filter by geography

- United States
- Canada
- UAE

### Step 4: Prioritize signals

- Active blog or resource center
- Active LinkedIn company page
- Case studies page
- Webinar or events page
- Product launch activity
- Hiring for content, SEO, social, product marketing, demand gen, growth, or marketing ops roles
- Lean team with visible marketing activity
- Consistent publishing but signs of limited internal bandwidth
- Founder-led or small team with clear demand generation intent

### Step 5: Exclude bad fits

- 1000+ employee enterprise unless unusually strong fit
- No visible marketing activity
- Very early startups with no traction
- Translation / localization businesses
- Companies where influencer marketing is the clearest pain point
- Creator economy companies where creator partnerships are the primary need
- Businesses that clearly need mainly paid ads or full in-house video production

### Step 6: Find contacts

- Pull people in:
  - Marketing
  - Growth
  - Content
  - SEO
  - Social
  - Brand
  - Product Marketing
  - Marketing Operations
  - RevOps
- Prioritize seniority:
  - Founder
  - C-level
  - Head
  - VP
  - Director
  - Manager
- Exclude:
  - HR
  - Recruiting
  - Finance
  - Support
  - Junior coordinators
  - Pure sales roles

### Step 7: Assign service angle

- If active blog, SEO pages, or resource center:
  - Tag as Content Marketing or SEO Content
- If strong LinkedIn presence but inconsistent content distribution:
  - Tag as Social Media Execution
- If webinars, product launches, feature pages, or release announcements:
  - Tag as Product / Launch Marketing
- If hiring for ops, reporting, demand gen, or showing messy execution signals:
  - Tag as Marketing Ops / Reporting
- If agency or fractional marketing firm:
  - Tag as White-Label Agency Support
- If DTC brand with active social and email motion:
  - Tag as Social Media Execution or Creative Support

### Step 8: Score leads

- High priority:
  - Strong company fit + clear signal + right buyer title
- Medium priority:
  - Good company fit but weaker urgency signal
- Low priority:
  - Broad fit with no clear need or buying signal
