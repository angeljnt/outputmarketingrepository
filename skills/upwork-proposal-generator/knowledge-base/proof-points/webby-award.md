# Webby Award

## Summary

- Reusable flagship proof point.
- Best framed as a campaign Vanessa designed and executed.
- The work was showcased in Times Square.
- URL is verified and reusable.

## Safe framing

- "I recently won a Webby Award for a campaign I designed and executed that got showcased in Times Square."
- "That matters here because the work had to be strong strategically and strong enough creatively to stand out in a crowded B2B space."

## URLs

- https://winners.webbyawards.com/2025/advertising-media-pr/pr-campaigns/best-b2b-campaign/340323/archivecom--big-brand-hijack

## Avoid

- Adding extra achievement claims not backed by the URL.
- Pairing it with too many other proof points in a short proposal.

## Notes

- Use whenever it strengthens credibility, but do not force it into every brief.
