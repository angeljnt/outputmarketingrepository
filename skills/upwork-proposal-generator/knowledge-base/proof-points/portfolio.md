# Portfolio

## Summary

- Main reusable proof hub for career highlights.
- Covers Archive.com, Apollo.io, and related work.
- Safe to include when the client will likely want samples or background quickly.

## Safe framing

- "If you want a cleaner view of the kind of work I do, my portfolio is here: https://portfoliovanessa.lovable.app/"
- "It gives you the fastest overview of the range and quality of my work."
