# No-Code and Tool Obsession

## Summary

- Comfortable with Airtable, Zapier, n8n, Webflow, Lovable, and Notion.
- Strong fit when a client needs systems, automation, or content operations.
- Can be light and human in tone rather than sounding like a stack list.

## Safe framing

- "I'm a little obsessed with no-code tools because they make good marketing faster and less chaotic."
- "I've worked with tools like Airtable, Zapier, n8n, Webflow, Lovable, and Notion, so I can usually build a cleaner workflow instead of just adding more manual work."
- "I've even sold Notion templates to friends and colleagues."
