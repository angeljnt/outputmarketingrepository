# AI-Enhanced Marketing

## Summary

- Started with Claude and ChatGPT like most marketers.
- Now uses Claude Code and Codex with marketing repositories.
- Focus is consistency across messaging and assets.
- Strong differentiator for clients who care about leverage without losing quality.

## Safe framing

- "I started with Claude and ChatGPT like everyone else, but now I use Claude Code and Codex with marketing repositories to keep messaging and assets consistent."
- "That means you're getting state-of-the-art AI-enhanced marketing without the usual AI slop."
- "You're not getting someone who just prompts ChatGPT and calls it a day. You're getting someone building systems that make AI actually useful for marketing."
- "I use AI to increase consistency, speed, and quality across assets, not to flood channels with generic copy."
