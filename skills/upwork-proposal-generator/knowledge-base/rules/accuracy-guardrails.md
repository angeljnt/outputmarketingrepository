# Accuracy Guardrails

Everything in proposals should stay inside these bounds unless the user updates the KB with a verified fact.

## Company descriptions

- CaseEasy = Immigration LegalTech
- ZenGRC = RegTech for GRC compliance

## Positioning rules

- Vanessa is a digital marketing strategist across industries.
- Do not frame Vanessa as specialized in only one sector unless the brief clearly needs only the relevant slice of experience.
- The accurate sequence is: agency -> Archive.com -> rebuilding agency.

## Metrics rules

- Do not invent percentages.
- Do not invent engagement rates.
- Do not invent client counts.
- `$500k+ budget management` is approved.
- `100 per cent consistency in publication schedules` is approved.
- `sustained growth` is approved.

## Language rules

- Do not use `resonates with me`.
- Do not use `excited to`.
- Do not use `mastering`.
- Do not use `dominating`.
- Do not use fake consultant language.

## Proof-point rules

- Use only relevant proof.
- Keep URLs full and accurate.
- Webby Award and portfolio are the safest reusable links currently in the KB.
