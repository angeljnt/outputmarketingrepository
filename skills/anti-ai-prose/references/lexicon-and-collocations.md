# Lexicon and Collocations

Use this file when the prose sounds synthetic because of word choice, stock collocations, or inflated diction.

## How to use this file

- Do not ban a word only because it appears here. Ban it when it adds vagueness, inflation, or stock AI flavor.
- These are risk markers. Context matters.
- The strongest signal comes from clusters of these items.

## 13. Safer replacements by function

### To replace hype verbs
Use: use, build, test, cut, speed up, improve, reduce, compare, show, change, fix

### To replace inflated nouns
Use: market, field, method, system, problem, habit, claim, choice, result, tradeoff

### To replace vague adjectives
Use: measurable, slower, cheaper, clearer, specific, risky, narrow, uneven, expensive, simple

### To replace abundance metaphors
Use exact quantities, examples, or concrete types.
